About Vegas

Vegas is one of the most popular tourist sites or vacation spot in all of America and especially in Nevada. It is has the most populated city in Nevada and the 28th in all of America. There is many things to do in Vegas and they are mostly known for its gambling, shopping, fine dining, entertainment, and nightlife. Some fun facts about the city of Las Vegas is as following

  • There is estimated to be at least 1,000 people living beneath Vegas in underground tunnels
  • It would take 288 years for one person to spend one night in every hotel room in Las Vegas
  • The Las Vegas Strip is the brightest place on Earth when looked at from outer space
  • 15 of the worlds top 25 hotels are located in Las Vegas
