Paris, Capital of France

About Paris

Paris is the capital of france and has an estimated 17.95 million tourists each year. It is by far the countrys most important center of commerce and culture. Home to historical monuments such as the Eiffel tower , Bastille, Louvre and before it burned down the famous Norte Dame. It has a population of 2 million people in which compared to the tourists it is out numbered.

Fun Facts about Paris

  • There is only on stop sign in Paris
  • Walking from the north to the south of the city takes 2 hours
  • It is one of the fashion capitals in the world
  • The Louvre is the worlds biggest art museum
