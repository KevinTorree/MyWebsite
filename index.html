About Acapulco

Acacpulco is located at Mexico's paciific coast and is home to one of the most famus beaches in all of mexico. This famous site is the most popular touring site in all of Mexico and besides being known for their beches they have a variety of other fun activities. For example the seaside cliff "La Quebrada" or the historical mueseum of Fort San Diego.
